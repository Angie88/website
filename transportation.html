Skytrain

It takes you straight to Downtown with only $8.00 dolars, and it comes every 3 min.

Bus

The price of the fare of Vancouver's public transportation is 2.75, it last for 1.5 hours and by bus you can use it in all Great Vancouver area.

Recently the compass card system was added in the city. It is a multiple use card that can be reload with monthly pass, day pass day or value from 10.00 dollars. If you use a compass card the fare decreases to 2.10. It is also use in skytrain and seabus

The system is quite good you can keep track of your buses through different apps and also by phone call or sending a text with your stop number to 33-333, text is one of the most accuaretes.
